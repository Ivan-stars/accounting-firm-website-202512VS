Audit & Assurance

We help Hong Kong businesses present clear, credible financial information. As HKICPA-licensed auditors, we follow Hong Kong Standards on Auditing and Assurance to deliver independent reports for statutory audits, internal control reviews, and forecast assurance. Our team knows Companies Ordinance timelines and regulatory expectations, so audits run on schedule, meet tax and filing needs, and strengthen transparency and investor confidence.

Statutory Audit

We perform statutory audits under HKICPA standards and IFRS. Our process spans planning, control assessment, substantive testing, and reporting so financial statements fairly reflect the business and meet Companies Ordinance deadlines. We tailor our work for startups and established groups, improving transparency and trust with regulators, investors, and banks.

Internal Audit

We design internal audits aligned to IIA standards and Hong Kong rules. Beyond finance, we review operational, compliance, IT, and strategic risks, testing controls and processes to spot gaps early. Findings include practical fixes that strengthen governance, risk prevention, and board oversight for sustainable growth.

Special Purpose Audit

For deals, loans, subsidies, or due diligence, we deliver focused audits built for the specific transaction. We follow HKICPA standards while balancing speed and quality, providing reliable evidence that reduces risk and supports negotiations or financing in Hong Kong’s fast-moving market.

IPO Preparation Audit

Our IPO team knows HKEX rules and works end-to-end—from early financial due diligence and control reviews to listing document support. We emphasise both historic accuracy and post-listing compliance, coordinating with sponsors and regulators so companies address review points quickly and build investor confidence ahead of listing.

Tax Planning

We optimise tax structures while staying compliant locally and internationally, helping clients lower tax costs and stay audit-ready. Our comprehensive tax solutions cover corporate tax planning, cross-border tax planning, personal tax advisory, and tax compliance services. We design efficient structures that align with Hong Kong Inland Revenue Ordinance incentives and double tax treaties, balance transfer pricing, and time income and expenses to improve cash flow. For executives and high-net-worth clients, we tailor compensation, residency, and succession plans. We also handle profits, salaries, and stamp duty filings, monitor Inland Revenue Department updates, and prepare responses during queries to minimise penalties.

Corporate Tax Planning

We use Hong Kong’s low-tax regime and Inland Revenue Ordinance incentives to design efficient structures, time income and expenses, and align transfer pricing with global rules. Plans stay compliant while improving cash flow for businesses of all sizes.

Cross-border Tax Planning

Leveraging Hong Kong’s double tax treaties, we map efficient routes for investments, trading, and IP. We align transfer pricing with BEPS, coordinate with the Inland Revenue Department and overseas advisors, and keep clients updated on rule changes to cut cross-border tax costs and risks.

Personal Tax Advisory

For executives and high-net-worth clients, we plan salaries tax, offshore income, residency implications, and succession. We tailor compensation and benefit structures for expatriates and cross-border roles so they remain compliant while optimising after-tax income.

Tax Compliance Services

We handle profits, salaries, and stamp duty filings, monitor IRD updates, and run periodic tax health checks. When queries arise, we prepare responses and documentation to minimise penalties and disputes, keeping businesses compliant as transparency requirements rise.

Business Consulting

We provide practical advice on strategy, finance, and operations so your business can grow confidently in competitive markets. Our strategic business solutions cover business structure planning, business plan development, financial management optimization, and business process improvement. We design Hong Kong–appropriate group and shareholding structures that balance tax benefits, regulatory needs, and management efficiency. We craft concise, investor-ready plans that highlight Hong Kong and Asia-Pacific opportunities, streamline reporting and controls aligned with HKFRS/IFRS, and set up budgeting and cost control. Using best-practice process analysis, we pinpoint bottlenecks and redesign workflows for regional headquarters and cross-border teams while keeping global standards consistent.

Business Structure Planning

We design Hong Kong–appropriate group and shareholding structures that balance tax benefits, regulatory needs, and management efficiency. Plans support mainland firms going global and multinationals using Hong Kong as a hub, reducing costs and clarifying governance.

Business Plan Development

We craft concise, investor-ready plans covering market analysis, positioning, financials, and risks, highlighting Hong Kong and Asia-Pacific opportunities. The focus is securing financing for startups, tech, and cross-border projects.

Financial Management Optimization

We build reporting and control frameworks aligned with HKFRS/IFRS, streamline processes, and set up budgeting and cost control. For listed or multinational firms, we emphasise compliance, transparency, and training to strengthen decision-ready finance teams.

Business Process Improvement

Using best-practice process analysis, we pinpoint bottlenecks and redesign workflows for efficiency and cost control. For regional HQs in Hong Kong, we align cross-border processes with local regulations and culture while keeping global standards consistent.

Risk Management

We help organisations spot, evaluate, and mitigate risks so they can operate securely and keep growing. Our comprehensive risk solutions cover risk assessment, internal control review, compliance monitoring, and risk mitigation strategies. We map strategic, financial, operational, and compliance risks into clear ratings and heat maps, design COSO-based controls aligned with Hong Kong regulatory expectations, and run compliance scans across securities, banking, insurance, data privacy, and AML. We build and rehearse crisis plans—especially for social media and reputation risk—supporting incident response, communications, and recovery so leadership can act with confidence and resume operations quickly.

Risk Assessment

We apply structured methodologies to map strategic, financial, operational, and compliance risks, creating clear ratings and heat maps. Recommendations reflect Hong Kong’s market and regulatory realities, giving leadership a solid basis for decisions and resource allocation.

Internal Control Review

Based on COSO and local regulatory expectations, we design and test control frameworks, manuals, and key control points. Work is tailored for listed and regulated entities so controls prevent risks and support business growth.

Compliance Monitoring

We monitor requirements across securities, banking, insurance, data privacy, and AML. Services include risk scans, training, and periodic reviews, with guidance for mainland and international firms entering Hong Kong to stay compliant and avoid penalties.

Risk Mitigation Strategies

We build and rehearse crisis plans covering prevention, response, and communications, with special focus on social media and reputation risk in Hong Kong. During incidents we support media handling and recovery so operations resume quickly.
